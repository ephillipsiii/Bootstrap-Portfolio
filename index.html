About Me

Hi, there. My name is Edford Phillips and I'm an aspiring web developer. Drawing from a wide variety of experiences, from teaching High School, working in a homebrew shop, and currently working in a production brewery on their packaging line, I'm learning how to build responsive, user-friendly websites at the University of Denver Coding Bootcamp.

My passions are varied and many. I enjoy camping and hiking, playing and listening to music, video games, cooking and homebrewing (I'm known to try and ferment just about anything!), and reading thousand page plus epic fantasy novels - along with just about anything else I can get my hands on. Take a few minutes to poke around the site and view some of my projects (upcoming!) and drop me a line if you see something that catches your eye!
